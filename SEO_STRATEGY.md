## üí° Pro Tips for Top 10 Ranking

### Do's ‚úÖ
1. **Focus on user intent** - Answer questions people actually ask
2. **Long-form content** - 2000+ word blog posts rank better
3. **Update content regularly** - Google loves fresh content
4. **Build quality backlinks** - 1 DA60 link > 100 DA10 links
5. **Optimize for featured snippets** - Use Q&A format
6. **Mobile-first** - 60% of searches are mobile
7. **E-A-T** - Expertise, Authority, Trust
8. **Local SEO** - If applicable, optimize for local searches

### Don'ts ‚ùå
1. **Keyword stuffing** - Natural language only
2. **Buying links** - Google penalty risk
3. **Duplicate content** - Unique content only
4. **Neglecting technical SEO** - Foundation matters
5. **Ignoring competitors** - Learn from top rankers
6. **Black hat tactics** - Short-term gain, long-term pain
7. **Forgetting about users** - SEO for humans, not bots
